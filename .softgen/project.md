# Micucci Electrical Services - Website

## Vision
Professional landing page for Micucci Electrical Services, a 5-star rated electrician in Mill Park, Victoria. Designed to convert visitors into customers through trust signals (24/7 availability, 5.0 Google rating), clear service offerings, and prominent contact forms.

## Design
**Colors:**
- `--primary: 217 91% 60%` (electric blue - trust, energy)
- `--primary-foreground: 0 0% 100%` (white text on blue)
- `--secondary: 222 47% 11%` (deep navy - professionalism)
- `--accent: 38 92% 50%` (amber - urgency, CTAs)
- `--background: 0 0% 100%` (clean white)
- `--foreground: 222 47% 11%` (navy text)
- `--muted: 214 32% 91%` (light blue grey)
- `--muted-foreground: 215 16% 47%` (muted text)

**Typography:**
- Heading font: Space Grotesk (geometric, tech-inspired)
- Body font: Outfit (clean, modern sans-serif)
- Style: Bold headlines, ample whitespace, professional

## Features
1. Hero section with prominent contact form and 24/7 availability badge
2. Services section showcasing electrical expertise
3. Why Choose Us section highlighting trust signals (5.0 rating, 24/7, licensed)
4. Gallery section with project photos
5. FAQ section addressing common electrical concerns
6. Final CTA section with contact details
